frequently asked questions

how to cancel?

You can cancel your reservation by logging into your account and selecting the booking you want to cancel, or by contacting our front desk directly via phone or email.

is there any vacancy?

Availability depends on your selected dates. Please check our online booking section or call our front desk for real-time updates on available rooms.

what are payment methods?

We accept major credit/debit cards, online bank transfers, GCash, and cash payments at the front desk.

how to claim coupons codes?

Apply your valid coupon code during checkout on our website or mention it during your booking call to enjoy exclusive discounts.

what are the age requirements?

Guests must be at least 18 years old to make a reservation. Children under 12 stay for free when sharing a room with parents or guardians.
